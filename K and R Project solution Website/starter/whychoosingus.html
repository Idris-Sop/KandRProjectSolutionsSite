what we offer

Translation / Interpretation Service

K & R Project Solutions is a premier conference or on-field interpreting service provider throughout Africa, with a strong presence in key cities such as Johannesburg, Pretoria, Durban, and Cape Town. With over four years of experience, we specialise in delivering highly effective interpreting solutions tailored for all sizes of international conferences, meetings, and seminars. Our services encompass a range of interpreting styles, including simultaneous, consecutive, and whispered interpreting, ensuring we meet the diverse needs of our clients and their events. Our interpreters are fully bilingual and possess a deep understanding of cultural nuances, enabling them to facilitate clear and precise communication for seamless interactions among participants. We take pride in our team's extensive experience in supporting events focused on significant themes such as gender equality, women's empowerment, and social change, both across Africa and globally. Our commitment to these causes enhances our ability to provide interpreting services that are accurate, sensitive, and relevant to the context of the discussions. K & R Project Solutions is dedicated to helping our clients convey their messages effectively while fostering inclusive dialogues that promote positive change.

Remote Simultaneous Interpretation for multilingual meetings

Social distancing does not mean we cannot stay connected with our virtual interpreters in Africa. Social distancing and remote work are essential to global efforts to combat the pandemic, leading to a shift from physical meetings to safer, more flexible online environments. Many commercial enterprises, private organisations, and NGOs in South Africa use virtual conferencing platforms such as Teams, Google Meet, Skype, and Zoom. K & R Project Solutions can facilitate web-based software, supported by UN-standard professional interpreters with specialised subject knowledge, to provide online simultaneous interpretation for your delegates worldwide. We possess the expertise to deliver accurate, experienced, and accessible simultaneous interpreting solutions for virtual meetings.


Our experienced Zoom interpreters offer simultaneous interpretation for your business conferences, webinars, meetings, or any other business event broadcasted to multiple countries in different languages. In South Africa, multinational, regional, and international organisations often face linguistic diversity in their communications with staff and external partners. Major official languages include English, French, Spanish, Portuguese, German, and Zulu. Contact us to get started.
